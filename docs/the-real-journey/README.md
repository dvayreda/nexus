## Why This Exists

Most entrepreneurship content is bullshit:
- "I made $100K in 60 days!"
- "Just follow my 3-step system!"
- "It's so easy!"

Reality is messy:
- Things break
- Learning takes time
- Perfectionism slows you down (but makes it better)
- Infrastructure before profit
- Two steps forward, one step back

**This documents THE REAL PROCESS.**

So when someone reads this in 2 years, they see:
- What actually worked
- What failed
- How long things REALLY took
- The decision points
- The multiple paths
- The trade-offs

And they can learn from the REAL journey, not the highlight reel.
