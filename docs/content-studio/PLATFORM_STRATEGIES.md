### Design Best Practices

**Color Psychology:**
- Red/Orange: Urgency, excitement (food, sports)
- Blue: Trust, calm (technology, business)
- Purple: Creative, luxury (art, fashion)
- Green: Growth, health (wellness, nature)

**Typography:**
- Primary Font: Bold, sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins)
- Size: 60-80pt for headers, 40-50pt for body
- Contrast: Always readable at thumbnail size
- Hierarchy: Clear visual flow

**Layout:**
- 1:1 Aspect Ratio (1080x1080px)
- Safe Zone: 10% margins on all sides
- Consistent template across slides
- Maximum 2-3 visual elements per slide

### Video Production Tips

**Equipment:**
- Smartphone camera is enough
- Ring light for face-to-camera
- Tripod for stability
- External mic for better audio (optional)

**Editing:**
- Fast cuts every 2-3 seconds (holds attention)
- On-screen text for key points (80% watch with sound off)
- Zoom-ins on important moments
- Transitions on beat with music

**Text Overlays:**
- Large, bold font
- Yellow/white for readability
- Appear word-by-word (dynamic)
- Highlight key words in different color

### Trending Audio Strategy

**Why It Matters:**

- Monitor competitors' successful posts
